2.A Personal Information from Schools

As stated above, all of the personal information that we collect and store is provided to us either by a School on behalf of the individuals associated with that School’s account, or by a School-Created User Account visiting and using our Services under or in connection with a School account.

A few examples of how and when this may happen include:

  • a School submits personal information to a School account and sets up School-Created User Accounts for teachers, administrators, staff, parents, guardians, or students, and includes personal information in setting up those School-Created User Accounts; 
  • a School-Created User Account, on behalf of the School in her or his capacity as a School employee, accesses, uploads, uses, and/or otherwise interacts with Thrillshare and/or Rooms and includes personal information in her or his use;
  • a School-Created User Account, on behalf of the School in her or his capacity as a School employee, submits personal information about one or more individuals on or via Thrillshare or Rooms by uploading and publishing content that includes personal information, or by sending communications that include personal information;
  • a School-Created User Account, on behalf of the School in her or his capacity as a School employee, interacts with us with respect to maintaining the School’s account or for customer service (for example: technical support) and includes personal information in her or his communications; 
  • a School-Created User Account assigned to a student utilizes the comment or chat function, or assignment submission function of Rooms and includes personal information in her or his use; or
  • a School User does any of the foregoing actions in her or his individual capacity under or in connection with her or his School’s account.

In any and all of these instances, the School determines what personal information is and can be provided to us by School-Created User Accounts, and what can be collected by us, under the School’s account. In other words, the School is the data controller for the personal information that is provided and collected in connection with the School’s account with us.

Because of that, your School is the party that is primarily responsible for the personal information that we collect in connection with your School’s account. And your School is the party that is primarily responsible for protecting the privacy of your personal information, and whether and how it is made available on our Services.

In any and all of these circumstances, Apptegy is only a data processor. This means that we only carry out and process, via our Services, the activities of and instructions from your School (and/or the School-Created User Accounts using Services under the School’s account).

Simply put: when we process activities and instructions from a School that include personal information about you (or by you or another School-Created User Account under or in connection with a School account), we are not responsible for the disclosures made by the School (or School-Created User Account under or in connection with the School account). It is your School that has the responsibility to protect your privacy.

Please note that the privacy and security policies of your School may differ from our policies. In those cases, our policies will not overrule or change the separate policies of your School, and our policies will not impact or change your School’s separate agreements with and obligations to you. Instead, the privacy and security policies of your School govern your personal information and your rights, and not our policies. As such, you are required to work with your School directly with respect to your personal information.

Even though we are not primarily responsible for your personal information in these circumstances, we still want you to understand what personal information we might collect about you under these circumstances and how we collect and protect that information.

Schools (and/or School-Created User Accounts using our Services under or in connection with School accounts) use our Services to:

For Rooms, the information submitted is restricted to other School-Created User Accounts with specific permission to access or view a particular classroom(s) at the School. As explained above, Schools (and/or the School-Created User Accounts using our Services under or in connection with School accounts) make those choices and are responsible for them.
